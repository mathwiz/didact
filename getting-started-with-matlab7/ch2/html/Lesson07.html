Simple Spiral Plot

Let a spiral be given by r(t) = exp(-theta/10), 0<=theta<=10*pi)

Create vectors theta and r

theta = linspace(0, 10*pi, 200);
r = exp(-theta/10);

Plot theta vs r in polar

polar(theta, r)
